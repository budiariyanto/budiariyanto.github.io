I Remove CBox Widgets From My Blog

Jan 29th, 2011

I decide to remove my CBox widget on my blog because the spams still come to it even I have turn on the spam protection on my CBox configuration. Actually, it’s a great tool for make a communication for people that I don’t know since I and another person not have to make an account. Maybe I’ll wait for CBox to improve it’s security so, hopefully I want to add it again in my blog widgets.
